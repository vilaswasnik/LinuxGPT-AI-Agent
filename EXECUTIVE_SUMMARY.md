# LinuxGPT - Executive One-Pager
## AI-Powered Linux Command Assistant

---

## WHAT IS IT?
Natural language interface that converts plain English into Linux commands. Ask questions in your own words, get instant results.

**Example:**  
You type: *"show me files larger than 100MB"*  
LinuxGPT executes: `find . -type f -size +100M -exec ls -lh {} \;`
